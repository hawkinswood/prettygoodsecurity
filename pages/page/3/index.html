The Weakest Link: Understanding Your Digital Security Chain Your digital security operates as an interconnected system where the protection of one account often depends on the security of others. Just like a physical chain, your security is only as strong as its weakest link. Understanding these relationships helps you make informed decisions about where to prioritize your security efforts for maximum protection. Why Email Accounts Are Your Security Foundation Most digital services are designed around email addresses as the primary identifier and recovery method.
